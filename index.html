John Conway's Game of Life

Spacebar to pause/unpase
Up and down arrows to change grid size (minimum 10x10)
Left and right arrows to change framerate
Backspace to clear the board (while paused)
Enter to go forward one generation (one frame). Only works when paused.

1-9 keys (not on the numpad) create different patterns. There are more patterns listed on the wikipedia page for the game of life, as well as on the online game of life's lexicon page
