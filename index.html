John Conway's Game of Life

When paused, you can toggle cells on and off by clicking them. Spacebar: pause/unpase
Up and down arrows: change grid size, minimum 10x10 (up=increase size, down=decrease)
Left and right arrows: change framerate (right=higher, left=lower)
Backspace or delete: clear the grid (while paused)
Tilde (~, but without the shift, so actually a backquote `): randomize the state of the grid Enter to go forward one generation (one frame). Only works when paused.

1-9 keys (not on the numpad) create different patterns. There are more patterns listed on the wikipedia page for the game of life, as well as on the online game of life's lexicon page
